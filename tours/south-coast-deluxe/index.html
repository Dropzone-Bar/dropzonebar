SOUTH COAST DELUXE

The south coast is spectacular and shows us how great nature really can be.

We travel along the south coast of Iceland and make stops at one of the most beautiful waterfall you will ever see, Seljalandsfoss has a little cave behind that is accessible and we will walk behind the waterfall.

Proceeding forward we make our way to the glacier Solheimajokull where we will see the effects of global warming in person, maybe we'll even take a hike on the glacier!

Next stop is the black sand beach Reynisfjara, where roaring atlantic waves hit the rock formations with great force.
